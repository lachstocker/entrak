COMMERCIAL AGREEMENT FOR SERVICE PROVISION

BETWEEN:
Acme Corporation ("Client")
AND
Global Services Ltd. ("Provider")

EFFECTIVE DATE: March 25, 2025

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
1.1 Provider agrees to provide the following services to Client:
   (a) Software development and maintenance
   (b) Technical support and troubleshooting
   (c) System integration services

2. TERM AND TERMINATION
2.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of 24 months.
2.2 Provider must submit renewal terms in writing 90 days before expiration.
2.3 Client may terminate this Agreement upon 30 days written notice if Provider fails to meet service levels.
2.4 Provider shall deliver all final deliverables within 14 days of termination.

3. PAYMENT TERMS
3.1 Client shall pay Provider a monthly fee of $5,000, due within 30 days of invoice receipt.
3.2 Late payments will incur interest at a rate of 1.5% per month.
3.3 Provider shall submit detailed invoices on the 1st of each month.
3.4 Client shall reimburse Provider for pre-approved expenses within 15 days of submission.

4. PERFORMANCE STANDARDS
4.1 Provider must maintain 99.9% uptime for all critical systems.
4.2 Provider shall respond to critical issues within 2 hours and resolve within 8 hours.
4.3 Provider must submit monthly performance reports by the 5th of the following month.
4.4 Client will conduct quarterly reviews of Provider's performance.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
5.1 All information shared during the course of this Agreement is deemed confidential.
5.2 Provider shall implement appropriate safeguards to protect Client's data.
5.3 Provider must notify Client within 24 hours of any data breach.
5.4 Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for 5 years.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
6.1 All work product created by Provider shall be owned exclusively by Client.
6.2 Provider shall execute any documents necessary to transfer IP rights to Client.
6.3 Provider warrants that its services will not infringe on third-party IP rights.
6.4 Provider must obtain written permission to use any third-party components.

7. COMPLIANCE
7.1 Provider shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations.
7.2 Provider must maintain all necessary licenses and certifications.
7.3 Provider shall submit compliance reports quarterly.
7.4 Client has the right to audit Provider's compliance at reasonable times.

8. INSURANCE
8.1 Provider shall maintain professional liability insurance of at least $2,000,000.
8.2 Provider must provide certificates of insurance annually.
8.3 Provider shall name Client as an additional insured on all policies.
8.4 Insurance coverage must be maintained throughout the term of the Agreement.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
9.1 All disputes shall first be addressed through good faith negotiation.
9.2 If negotiation fails, parties agree to binding arbitration.
9.3 Arbitration must be initiated within 30 days of dispute notification.
9.4 The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorney's fees.

10. MISCELLANEOUS
10.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties.
10.2 Any modifications must be in writing and signed by both parties.
10.3 Provider may not assign this Agreement without Client's written consent.
10.4 Notices must be in writing and delivered by registered mail or email with confirmation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the Effective Date.
